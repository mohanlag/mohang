Professional Certifications

Below are the certifications I have earned to enhance my technical skills and professional knowledge. These certifications validate my expertise in cybersecurity, cloud computing, and demonstrate my commitment to staying current with industry standards.

ISO 27001:2022 Lead Auditor

Issued by: CQI and IRCA

Date: 2024

Description: An ISO 27001:2022 Lead Auditor is a certified professional responsible for assessing an organization’s Information Security Management System (ISMS) against the ISO 27001:2022 standard. Their primary role involves planning, leading, and conducting audits to evaluate an organization's compliance with established information security practices, identifying areas for improvement, and ensuring that security measures align with ISO requirements.

Certified SOC Analyst (CSA)

Issued by: EC-Council

Date: 2022

Description: A Certified SOC Analyst (CSA) is a professional trained to monitor, detect, and respond to cybersecurity threats within a Security Operations Center (SOC). Their role is critical in defending an organization’s IT environment from cyber-attacks by identifying vulnerabilities and quickly addressing security incidents.

Certified Ethical Hacking (CEH)

Issued by: EC-Council

Date: 2021

Description: A Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) is a cybersecurity professional skilled in identifying and addressing vulnerabilities within computer systems and networks. By using the same techniques as malicious hackers, CEHs help organizations strengthen their defenses against potential cyber threats.

Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL)

Issued by: AXELOS

Date: 2015

Description:ITIL (Information Technology Infrastructure Library) is a framework of best practices designed to align IT services with the needs of the business. ITIL provides guidelines for delivering high-quality IT service management (ITSM) to optimize performance, enhance customer satisfaction, and streamline operations.

Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA)

Issued by: Cisco

Date: 2011

Description: The Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) certification validates a professional’s ability to install, configure, operate, and troubleshoot medium-sized networks. It covers essential networking concepts and skills needed to manage network infrastructure effectively.

Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT)

Issued by: Microsoft

Date: 2010

Description: A Microsoft Certified Trainer (MCT) is a qualified educator authorized to teach Microsoft certification courses. MCTs are experts in Microsoft technologies and are certified to deliver professional training on a variety of Microsoft platforms and tools.

These certifications represent my dedication to professional growth and staying updated with technological advancements. Each certification has provided me with valuable insights and skills that contribute to my ability to tackle complex challenges in my field.
